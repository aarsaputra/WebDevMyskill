Pengertian penetration Testing dari wikipedia 

  A
  penetration test, colloquially known as a pentest or ethical hacking, is an
  authorized simulated cyberattack on a computer system, performed to evaluate
  the security of the system;[1][2] this is not to be confused with a
  vulnerability assessment.[3] The test is performed to identify weaknesses (or
  vulnerabilities), including the potential for unauthorized parties to gain
  access to the system's features and data,[4][5] as well as strengths,[6]
  enabling a full risk assessment to be completed.
